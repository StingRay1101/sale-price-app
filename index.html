Sale Price App

Automatically discounts eligible items to a fixed sale price at checkout. No coupon code required.

Current Rule
Items priced under $49.96 are discounted to $25.00 at checkout.

To activate or deactivate the sale

  1. Go to Discounts in your Shopify admin
  2. Click Create discount then Discount app
  3. Select under-4996-sale-discount
  4. Set it to automatic, choose your dates, and save
